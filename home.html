Air Quality Prediciton

Air quality prediction is crucial for public health due to the increasing concern over pollution levels. In this study, the effectiveness of machine learning algorithms is explored in predicting the Air Quality Index (AQI) using a carefully selected subset of environmental features. The aim is to develop an efficient and accurate predictive model that can inform proactive measures to maintain air quality.

Random Forest is a powerful supervised learning algorithm renowned for its ability to handle complex datasets. By building multiple decision trees and aggregating their outputs, Random Forest improves prediction accuracy and stability. This ensemble method excels in both classification and regression tasks, making it a preferred choice for environmental data analysis.
